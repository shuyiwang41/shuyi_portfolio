    • About Me

    I am currently an engineering psychology student at Tufts University, with training in human factors, interaction design, and system-level thinking. My academic work sits at the intersection of human cognition, physical embodiment, and technological systems, with a particular focus on accessibility and inclusive design.

    I am interested in how design decisions shape who is supported, who is excluded, and how much agency users are given within everyday systems.
